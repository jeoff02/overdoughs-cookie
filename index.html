WHERE PASSION MEETS DOUGH

• We’re a student-founded startup, smashing the pastriarchy one cookie at a time. Created in 2023 by a group of hardworking students with a shared love for sweets and big dreams. Over Doughs began as a simple passion project that turned into something real — a cookie business built on creativity, confidence, and irresistible flavor. Every batch we bake represents who we are — bold, playful, and always craving better.
